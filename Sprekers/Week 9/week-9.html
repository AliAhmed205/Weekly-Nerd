Rosa Schuurman

About

During this Weekly Nerd session, we had Rosa Schuurman as a guest speaker. Rosa is known for her skills in UX design, React development, and her passion for 'creative coding'. She is a true pioneer in applying circuit bending, creatively adapting existing technologies and frameworks to create unique and innovative projects.

Circuit Bending

Rosa's expertise in circuit bending goes beyond just manipulating electronic devices. She uses this technique to discover and create new sounds, and has even contributed to projects such as sound design for ToysR'Us Studios. Her involvement in the circuit bending process illustrates her inventiveness and her ability to transform conventional technologies into something entirely new. By manipulating existing sound systems, Rosa uncovers new possibilities and brings fresh, creative perspectives to the forefront.

Alternative Game Development

In addition to her work in UX design and circuit bending, Rosa prefers alternative approaches to game development. Instead of traditional platforms like Unity, she opts for Gamemaker Studio, offering an alternative approach to game development. This choice reflects her creative spirit and willingness to think outside the box. By experimenting with different tools and frameworks, Rosa explores new possibilities and encourages others to embrace their own unique approaches.

Music Creation

Rosa's creative talent also extends to music creation, where she uses PureData, a visual programming language designed for interactive computer music and multimedia works. Her passion for music and technology converges in these creative expressions, resulting in unique and engaging compositions. By combining her UX design skills with her love for music, Rosa creates a seamless integration of technology and creativity that distinguishes and inspires her work.

Career Advice

During the session, Rosa shared valuable career advice, emphasizing the importance of surrounding oneself with inspiring people for professional growth. She advised interns to choose companies that align with their values and interests, and stressed the importance of developing hard skills to stand out in the job market.
