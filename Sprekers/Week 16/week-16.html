Workshop

Waag held a workshop during their visit and focused on exploring social values. Participants engaged in an exercise where they positioned themselves within one of four designated boxes on the floor, each corresponding to different responses to a series of questions. These questions touched on topics such as smartphone preference (Apple, Android, other), past switches between phone brands/systems, and considerations about privacy, data, and personal values.

Following this interactive exercise, participants worked in groups to fill out a form detailing their personal values regarding smartphones, reflecting on which values were currently met and which were not. Subsequently, participants embarked on a creative session to construct their ideal phone based on these values, using abstract hexagonal images as a design medium. Many designs prioritized safety as a central theme, reflecting participantsâ€™ collective aspirations for secure and value-aligned smartphone experiences.
