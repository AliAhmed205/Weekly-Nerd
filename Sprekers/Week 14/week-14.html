Robert Spier

About

Robert works as a Creative Technologist in the Digital Factory Team at Deloitte NL. He graduated from the Communication & Multimedia Design (CMD) program and also studied Information Systems at the University of Amsterdam. Today, he spoke about his background and experience, both personally and in relation to our team.

Creative Technologist

As a Creative Technologist, Robert focuses on various aspects. He excels in Front-End Development and conducts research on Emerging Technologies. His role also involves stakeholder management and communicating expectations to stakeholders. Robert is known for his ability to devise creative solutions to complex problems.

Robert utilizes a wide range of technologies in his work, including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Additionally, he works with tools like Vite and Svelte (Kit), switching between frameworks and platforms such as Angular, .NET, Node.js, and Azure. He integrates various RESTful APIs and npm modules to support his projects.

Robert's Development

Robert utilizes a wide range of technologies in his work, including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Additionally, he works with tools like Vite and Svelte (Kit). He switches between different frameworks and platforms such as Angular, .NET, Node.js, and Azure. He uses various RESTful APIs and npm modules to support his projects.

For his development work, Robert uses tools like Visual Studio Code and Github Desktop. One of his notable achievements is building an AI that acts as ChatGPT. He ensured that Deloitte does not subscribe to services that may grant access to sensitive business data to American companies, as this was not found to be attractive.
