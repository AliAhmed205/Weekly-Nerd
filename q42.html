Sense Math

Sense Math was a groundbreaking initiative by Q42 aimed at assisting blind students in interpreting mathematical graphs. By leveraging innovative technology, they developed an app that translates visual data into auditory cues. This approach significantly reduces the time students need to comprehend complex graphs, making mathematics more accessible and intuitive.
